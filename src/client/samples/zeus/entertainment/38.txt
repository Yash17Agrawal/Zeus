 Filmmaker Karan Johar says actor Suniel Shetty's son Ahan, who is reportedly in London for a special training for his debut film, is a 'star in the waiting'.
 
 
 
 
 Ahan, who is the brother of actress Athiya Shetty, will be soon making his acting debut in Bollywood with filmmaker Sajid Nadiadwala. Karan praised Ahan on Twitter on Monday.
 
 
 In full on training mode... A star in the waiting. Introducing Ahan Shetty. Congratulations to Sajid Nadiadwala and Suniel Shetty, Karan captioned a photograph which featured Ahan.
