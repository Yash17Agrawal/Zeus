Filmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali has redone the Chittorgarh fort on a set for the 'Ghoomar' song.TNN | Nov 28, 2016, 01.05 PM ISTFilmmaker Sanjay Leela Bhansali has redone the Chittorgarh fort on a set for the 'Ghoomar' song. The set has been inspired from actual references of the fort and Rajasthani paintings and diyas have been used as wall design. Around 400 diyas have been used to light the set. Chari dancers will also participate in the song being shot there. Deepika Padukone took an entire month to learn this dance form.
